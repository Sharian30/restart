Meet our Chief Information Officer (CIO)

Hi, I am Sharian, and I am the Chief Information Officer (CIO) of HealingHaven, a professional counseling platform dedicated to supporting girls and women struggling with depression. At HealingHaven, we offer personalized counseling services aimed at promoting mental health, self-empowerment, and emotional well-being. Our team of experienced professionals provides a safe, compassionate, and confidential space for healing and growth. We are committed to helping women navigate their challenges and find hope through expert guidance and care.

Our Journey & Accomplishments

Experience

Professional Experience and Expertise

As the CIO of HealingHaven since 2023, I have been leading the initiative to provide professional mental health counseling specifically designed for women and girls. Prior to this, I spent over five years offering private counseling services, during which I provided emotional and mental health support to women from diverse backgrounds. My experience also includes facilitating group therapy sessions, where I conducted workshops focused on empowerment and self-healing, helping participants navigate through their challenges and build resilience.

Achievements

Key Achievements

I became a Certified Mental Health Professional in 2022 and, since then, have successfully counseled over 1,000 clients, helping them on their journey to emotional well-being. My innovative counseling approaches have been featured in Women’s Health Magazine, further establishing my credibility in the field. Additionally, I was honored with the 2023 Social Impact Award for my significant contributions to women's mental health in the community.
